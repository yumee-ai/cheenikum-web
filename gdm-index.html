Questions & Answers

What is Gestational Diabetes? +
Gestational Diabetes occurs during pregnancy and typically resolves after childbirth. Proper monitoring and management are essential for you and your baby’s health.
Is the app free? +
Yes, basic features such as logging and meal planning are free. We also offer a Premium plan with advanced AI insights and teleconsultations.
How can I add my doctor’s advice or insulin plan? +
You can update your settings to reflect your doctor’s recommended blood sugar ranges and insulin doses. The app adjusts alerts based on those custom values.
What about postpartum usage? +
We encourage postpartum monitoring for a few weeks. You can also track general health and receive reminders for postpartum diabetes screenings.
Who can I contact for help or feature requests? +
Reach us through our Help Center. We value user feedback and feature suggestions.
